# Example: Service Business Brand

Sample run of `brand-launchpad` on a fictional voice-agent agency for
small businesses called **DialThread** (AI receptionist for clinics + salons).

> All names, numbers, and outputs are illustrative only.

---

## Step 0: Capture

```yaml
project_name: dialthread
brand_type: service
real_name_or_alias: founder's real name on LinkedIn; "@dialthread.studio" on IG
one_sentence_what_it_does: "AI receptionist that picks up before the third ring for clinics and salons."
icp:
  who: Single-location clinic owners and salon owners with 1 front-desk hire
  pain_or_desire: Missing 30% of calls during peak hours, losing bookings to competitors
  where_they_hang_out: LinkedIn (B2B owners), local WhatsApp groups, owner-operator subreddits
desired_outcome_12mo: 20 paying clinics on retainer
constraints:
  anonymous_required: no
  time_per_week_hours: 8
  camera_comfort: yes
  platforms_committed: [LinkedIn, Instagram]
```

## Step 4b: Two-handle architecture

| Handle | Role |
|--------|------|
| **Founder's real name on LinkedIn** | Polished face. B2B carousels, customer-deployment teardowns. |
| **`@dialthread.studio` on IG** | Public lab notebook. Real call clips (with permission), cost screenshots, tracebacks. |

## Step 6c: Named recurring series

1. **"Why this clinic switched"** — Friday LinkedIn carousel. Real
   customer migration only.
2. **"Cost per booked call"** — Biweekly Wednesday number breakdown.
   Real bills only.
3. **"Calls the AI can't handle"** — Every 3 weeks Monday contrarian.
   The 12% of calls that still need a human, by category.
4. **"Before / after at [client name]"** — Monthly long-form deep dive.
   Requires customer consent.

## Step 9: Day-21 milestone

Land one clinic on a free 30-day pilot by Day 14. Capture call-handling
data Day 14 to 21. Week 4 content is 100% sourced from this one
deployment: real call clips, real bookings count, real owner reaction
video.
